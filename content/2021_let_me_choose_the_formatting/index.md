As a user, I want to be able to fully customize the way text is formatted.

This includes:

- Date
  - Order of date components
- Time
  - AM / PM
  - 12 / 24 hours
- DateTime
  - Time zone
  - Date - time separator
- First day of week
- Calendar
- Default temperature
- Currency (before, after, currency specific)
- Numbers
  - Thousands separator
  - Grouping style
- Displayed units (for each unit)
